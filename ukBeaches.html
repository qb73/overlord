Gold and Sword Beach

After the United States' landings, the British sent troops at 7:30 AM on June 6th. Codenamed Gold and Sword Beach. Gold's landing zones were How, Item, Jig, King. Sword's landing zones were Oboe, Peter, Queen, and Roger. Gold and Sword encountered little resistance for their sector, and generally those areas weren't as defended as Omaha. The defenses were light, and the Germans stationed there were defeated farily quickly. Gold's casualty rate was ~400, and Sword's casualty rate was ~630. Amn alternative area to invade was in Sword, where invading troops encountered multiple rivers they had to secure.
