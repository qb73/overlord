Juno Beach

Unlike the British and Americans the Canadians only had one sector, and at 8:00 AM, embarked as such. Juno's landing zones were Love, Mike, and Nan. Similar to Omaha Beach, heavy fortifications were built to combat the Canadians, and the frist wave took heavy casualties. From this encounter, they suffered 1,200 casualties, the second highest compared to Omaha and all other beaches.
